Our Story

Born from a Love of Sweetness

CheeseyCek started as a weekend obsession in a small Bangalore kitchen. Word spread, jars became boxes, and boxes became a boutique. Today we're a small team with one big purpose: bringing joy through honest, handcrafted desserts.

Who We Are

A Boutique Bakery with Soul

We are a small team of three — a pastry chef, a flavour dreamer, and a detail obsessive. Everything is made to order or in micro-batches, never sitting in a display case for too long.

We believe dessert should feel personal. So we keep it small, keep it fresh, and pour every bit of care into every crumb. Our no-bake cheesecakes are our pride — light yet indulgent, requiring no oven, just quality ingredients and patience.
